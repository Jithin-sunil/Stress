About Us

Welcome to MindLytics, a smart stress detection and mental wellness platform designed to help individuals understand and manage their stress levels effectively. Our platform combines technology and mental wellness support to provide users with a simple and accessible way to assess their stress levels through an interactive stress analysis system. Based on user responses and analysis, the system identifies stress levels and provides personalized recommendations to support mental well-being. We believe that mental health support should be easy to access. Therefore, our platform not only helps users understand their stress but also connects them with professional psychologists when needed. Users can explore recommended specialists, book appointments, and receive guidance tailored to their needs. In addition to professional support, we provide stress-relief activities and wellness exercises such as relaxation techniques, mindfulness activities, and self-care recommendations to help users reduce stress and improve daily well-being. Note: Our system is intended for stress screening and wellness support purposes only and does not replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment.
